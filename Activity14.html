INTERNET OF THINGS

The network of physical objects, gadgets, vehicles, buildings, and other items that are integrated with sensors, software, and connection, allowing them to collect and exchange data via the internet, is known as the Internet of Things (IoT). The IoT makes it possible to seamlessly combine the physical and digital worlds, opening up new possibilities for the automation, observation, and management of many systems and processes. Using a variety of wireless technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and cellular networks, the IoT enables objects to connect with one another and with central systems. These gadgets are capable of gathering environmental data, processing it locally or remotely, and then acting on the results.
